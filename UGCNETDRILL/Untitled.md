**Q.PA-01  Consider a demand curve which takes the form of a straight-line cutting both axes. Elasticity at the mid-point of the line would be**
(1) 0
(2) 1.0
(3) 1.5
(4) 2.0
*Correct Answer:* (2)
*Explanation:* The elasticity of demand on a linear demand curve is calculated using the formula: Lower Segment / Upper Segment. At the exact mid-point of the straight line, the length of the lower segment equals the upper segment, rendering a ratio of exactly 1.0.

**Q.PA-02  Price taker firms**
(1) Advertise to increase the demand for their product
(2) Do not advertise, because most advertising is wasteful.
(3) Do not advertise because they can sell as much as they want at the current price.
(4) Who advertise will get more profits than those who do not.
*Correct Answer:* (3)
*Explanation:* Under Perfect Competition, individual firms are price takers facing a horizontal perfectly elastic demand curve. At the prevailing price ceiling, they can sell an infinite volume of homogeneous output, removing any commercial justification for advertising.

**Q.PA-03  OPEC is an example of the type of producer’s organisation known as a**
(1) Marketing Board
(2) Producer’s Co-operative
(3) Trust
(4) Cartel
*Correct Answer:* (4)
*Explanation:* OPEC is the premier global example of a cartel—a formal agreement among sovereign producer nations to limit crude production, coordinate pricing structures, and split global market segments.

**Q.PA-09  People should be held accountable for their own behaviour, and if holding people accountable for their own behaviour entails capital punishment, then so be it. However, no person should be held accountable for behaviour of others over which he or she had no control. Which of the following is the most logical conclusion of the argument above ?**
